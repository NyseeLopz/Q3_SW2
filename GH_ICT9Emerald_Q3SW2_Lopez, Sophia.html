My Top 5 Movies of All Time!
Title: The Greatest Showman

Directed by: Michael Gracey

Synopsis: The plot follows the story of young P.T Barnum and his rise from nothing to the world famous circus director. He faces a series of obstacles on his way to success with his lack of money, his family problems and the drama associated with fame.

Released on: January 31, 2018 (Philippines)
Title: Train to Busan

Directed by: Sang-ho Yeon

Synopsis: A man (Gong Yoo), his estranged daughter and other passengers become trapped on a speeding train during a zombie outbreak in South Korea.

Released on: September 1, 2016 (Philippines)
Title: tick, tick... BOOM!

Directed by: Lin-Manuel Miranda

Synopsis: On the cusp of his 30th birthday, a promising young theater composer navigates love, friendship and the pressures of life as an artist in New York City.

Released on: November 19, 2021
Title: Mulan

Directed by: Tony Bancroft and Barry Cook

Synopsis: To save her father from death in the army, a young maiden secretly goes in his place and becomes one of China's greatest heroines in the process.

Released on: July 15, 1998 (Philippines)
Title: Spider-Man: No Way Home

Directed by: Jon Watts

Synopsis: With Spider-Man's identity now revealed, Peter asks Doctor Strange for help. When a spell goes wrong, dangerous foes from other worlds start to appear, forcing Peter to discover what it truly means to be Spider-Man.

Released on: January 8, 2022 (Philippines)
